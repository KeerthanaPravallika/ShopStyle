About the Company


ShopStyle is an Indian e-commerce company based in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. It was founded by Keerthana Pravallika in 2020. The company initially focused on fashion in future thought of expanding into other product categories such as consumer electronics, home essentials & groceries, and lifestyle products. It will be considered one of the big technology companies, along with Google, Apple, and Facebook . ShopStyle is known for its disruption of well-established industries through technological innovation and mass scale. It is fifth largest private employer in India. Our main motto is to reach different variety of dresses all over India. If you have any issues you can fill the form in Contact Us page.
